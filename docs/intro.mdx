# Introduction

## The Bot Built for Serious Discord Servers

Aether is a multipurpose Discord bot built specifically for gaming communities — with a deep focus on FiveM server management. Where other bots give you generic tools, Aether gives you a full control panel for your community, from member verification to live in-game commands, all in one place.

---

## What Aether Does

### Community Management
Keep your server safe and well-organized from day one.

- **Verification** — Gate access behind a button click or image captcha. Includes raid protection that automatically pauses joins if a flood is detected.
- **Welcome** — Greet new members with fully customizable messages and embeds. Supports DM welcomes.
- **Alt Prevention** — Automatically kick or ban accounts below a minimum age to stop alts before they enter.
- **Anti-Tagging** — Delete messages that mention protected roles (staff, ownership) and warn the sender.

### Moderation & Staff Tools
Give your staff the tools they need without bloat.

- **Strike Manager** — Issue, track, and act on strikes against members. Configure automatic actions at thresholds (warn, kick, ban). Full audit trail on every action.
- **User Notes** — Private staff-facing notes on members, never visible to the subject.
- **Purge** — Bulk delete messages from a channel, optionally filtered by user.
- **Suggestions** — Members submit suggestions; staff approve, deny, or mark as implemented with a reason.

### Role Management
Automate the tedious parts of running a large server.

- **Auto Roles** — Assign roles automatically when a member joins.
- **Sticky Roles** — Re-assign roles when a member who previously held them rejoins.
- **Reaction Roles** — Assign roles based on emoji reactions on a configured message.
- **Role Permissions** *(Gold)* — Let specific roles assign or remove other roles via commands, with full hierarchy and anti-escalation protection.
- **Vanity Roles** — Automatically reward members who include your Discord vanity invite in their status.

### Engagement
Keep your community active and rewarding.

- **Keyword Responder** — Auto-reply when configured keywords appear in a channel.
- **Booster Responder** — Celebrate server boosts with a configurable message and optional DM.
- **Echo** — Send messages or embeds as the bot in any channel.
- **Sticky Messages** — Pin a message to the bottom of a channel that re-posts whenever someone else sends.

### Tickets & Applications
Structured workflows for support and onboarding.

- **Tickets** — Full support ticket system with multiple panels, staff claiming, and transcripts. HTML transcripts and auto-transcribing on Diamond.
- **Applications** — Custom application forms for membership or staff roles, with accept/deny flows and applicant notifications.

### FiveM Integration *(Gold+)*
The feature no other general Discord bot has: a live connection to your FiveM server.

- See the live player list in Discord
- Look up detailed player info and live inventory snapshots
- Give items, heal players, kick, ban, and make server-wide announcements — all from Discord
- Connects automatically via a lightweight resource you install on your FiveM server

### Custom Instance *(Diamond)*
Your own dedicated bot, running under your branding. Set a custom bio, status, and activity. Fully isolated — your community, your identity.

## Getting Started

1. **Invite Aether** to your server using the invite link from your dashboard.
2. **Run `/settings`** in any channel to open the configuration menu.
3. **Select a module** from the menu and configure it using Discord's built-in modals and select menus.
4. **Manage everything** from the web dashboard at your panel URL.

No slash commands to memorize for setup. No bots to juggle. One `/settings` command controls everything.
