## Free — $0/month

Everything you need to run a clean, well-managed server. Free forever, no credit card required.

**Included:**
- All core modules (verification, welcome, moderation, suggestions, auto responders)
- 1 ticket panel, 3 application forms, 5 notes per member, 1 keyword responder
- 1 auto role, 5 reaction role panels, 5 sticky roles
- Vanity roles, alt prevention, anti-tagging, sticky messages, echo, purge

**Limits exist on** multi-entity modules (tickets, applications, etc.) to keep shared infrastructure healthy. Upgrade to Gold or Diamond to raise or remove them.

## Gold — $9.99/month

For active communities that need higher limits and the FiveM integration.

**Everything in Free, plus:**

### Higher Limits
- 3 ticket panels (vs 1)
- 5 application forms (vs 3)
- 10 notes per member (vs 5)
- 5 keyword responders (vs 1)
- 10 reaction role panels (vs 5)
- HTML ticket transcripts (vs text-only)

### Role Permissions Module *(Gold exclusive)*
Let your staff roles assign or remove specific roles via `/role add` and `/role remove` commands — without giving them full admin. Built-in protections prevent anyone from assigning roles above their own position or granting permissions they don't hold natively.

### FiveM Integration *(Gold exclusive)*
A live connection between your Discord server and your FiveM server. Once set up with a single `/fivem setup` command and a small resource on your FiveM server, your staff can:

- `/fivem playerlist` — see who's online right now
- `/fivem lookup <id>` — detailed player info, identifiers, job, money
- `/fivem inventory <id>` — live inventory snapshot
- `/fivem giveitem <id> <item> <amount>` — give items remotely
- `/fivem heal <id>` — heal a player to full (or a specified amount)
- `/fivem kick <id> [reason]` — kick a player with an optional reason
- `/fivem ban <id> [reason]` — ban a player
- `/fivem kickall [reason]` — disconnect everyone (e.g. before a restart)
- `/fivem announce <message>` — server-wide on-screen announcement

No RCON, no web panel, no third-party tools. Just Discord.

**Works with:** ESX, QBCore, QBox — detected automatically. Supports ox_inventory, qb-inventory, ps-inventory, and ESX default inventory.

## Diamond — $18.99/month

For communities that want Aether to feel like their own bot.

**Everything in Gold, plus:**

### Unlimited Limits
Every multi-entity limit is removed. Unlimited ticket panels, application forms, notes, keywords, reaction role panels, auto roles.

### Auto-Transcribing Tickets
Ticket transcripts are generated automatically when a ticket is closed — no staff action required. Delivered as formatted HTML to a configured channel.

### Custom Bot Instance
Your own Discord bot account, running under your server's branding. You bring the bot token; Aether runs it within the same hardened infrastructure. Your instance gets:

- Your own bot name, avatar, and bio (set via `/bot bio`)
- Your own status message (`/bot status`)
- Your own activity (`/bot activity`)
- All Aether modules, fully functional on your bot

**Your instance is fully isolated.** An error on another server's instance will never affect yours. If your instance crashes, it automatically reconnects with exponential backoff. If it can't reconnect, you're notified and your data is preserved.

**Data retention on lapse:** If your Diamond subscription lapses, your instance is shut down gracefully and all data is preserved for 30 days. Resubscribe within that window and everything is restored automatically.

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Can I upgrade or downgrade at any time?**
Yes. Upgrades take effect immediately. Downgrades take effect at the end of your current billing period, and your premium configuration is preserved for 30 days in case you come back.

**What happens if I cancel?**
Your server reverts to the Free tier at the end of the billing period. All data is preserved for 30 days. Nothing is deleted until that window passes.

**Is the FiveM integration hard to set up?**
No. Run `/fivem setup` in Discord, copy the token you receive, add it to a `config.lua` in the Aether FiveM resource, and start the resource. The resource auto-detects your framework and inventory system — no manual configuration.

**Does the Custom Instance share infrastructure with the main bot?**
It runs on the same server infrastructure but is fully isolated in code. Each instance has its own event handlers and command scope. One instance crashing does not affect any other.

**What payment methods do you accept?**
Payments are processed through Tebex, which supports major credit/debit cards and PayPal.
